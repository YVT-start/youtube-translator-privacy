Privacy Policy for YouTube Video Translator

Last updated: 19.11.2025

This Privacy Policy describes how YouTube Video Translator ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Our extension, YouTube Video Translator, is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information:

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, location, IP address, or any other personal data.
  • Video Information for Translation Only: We only access video information necessary for translation purposes, without linking it to any specific user, anonymously and without using any user data.
  • No User Tracking: We do not track your browsing history, search queries, or any online activities.
  • Local Storage Only: The extension only uses Chrome's local storage to save your personal preferences (like language settings) directly in your browser.

How We Use Information

The extension operates completely locally in your browser:

  • All translation processing happens locally in your browser
  • Your preferences (language settings, etc.) are stored locally using Chrome's storage API
  • No data is sent to any servers or third-party services

Data Storage

We do not store any data on our servers. The only information stored is:

  • Your extension preferences (language selection, settings) stored locally in your browser
  • This data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally

Permissions

Our extension requires the following permission:

  • Storage permission: Used only to store your translation preferences locally in your browser

Third-Party Services

Our extension does not use any third-party services, APIs, or external servers. All functionality operates locally within your browser.

Compliance with Children's Privacy

Our extension does not collect any personal information from children under 13 or any users of any age.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You can clear extension data by removing the extension from Chrome.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
