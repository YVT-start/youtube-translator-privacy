Privacy Policy for YouTube Video Translator

Last updated: 19.11.2025

This Privacy Policy describes how YouTube Video Translator ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our Chrome extension.

Transparency Note: We believe in being completely honest about what data we access and why.

Information We Process

To provide real-time translation of YouTube videos, our extension needs to:

  • Access Current Video Information: We process the video ID and current timestamp of the YouTube video you're watching to provide synchronized translations.
  • Anonymous Processing: This video information is processed anonymously and not linked to your identity.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, location, or IP address.

How We Use This Information

The video information is used solely for:

  • Identifying which video is being played to fetch appropriate translations
  • Synchronizing translations with the specific timestamp you're watching
  • Providing real-time AI-powered translation services

Data Storage and Retention

We do not permanently store any video viewing information. The video ID and timestamp data:

  • Is processed in real-time during your viewing session
  • Is immediately discarded after processing
  • Is not stored on our servers or associated with your identity

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage to save only your personal preferences:

  • Language selection preferences
  • Extension settings and configuration
  • This data remains locally in your browser

Third-Party Services

Our extension uses AI translation services. When you use the translation feature:

  • Video content may be sent to translation services solely for translation purposes
  • This is done anonymously without personal identifiers
  • We ensure these services adhere to data protection standards

Permissions

Our extension requires the following permissions:

  • YouTube access: Required to identify and translate video content
  • Storage permission: Used only to store your preferences locally in your browser

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to ensure:

  • Video data is processed anonymously without user identification
  • No persistent storage of video viewing patterns
  • Compliance with YouTube's Terms of Service

Compliance with Children's Privacy

Our extension does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

Since we don't store personal data, there's no personal information to access or delete. You can stop using our extension at any time.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
