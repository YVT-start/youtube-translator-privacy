Privacy Policy for YouTube Video Translator

Last updated: 2025-01-01

This Privacy Policy explains how the Chrome Extension YouTube Video Translator (“we”, “our”, “the Extension”) handles user data.

1. Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, store, share, or transfer any personal data.

All processing (subtitle reading, translation processing, UI display) happens locally on the user’s device.

2. Permissions Used

The Extension may request the following permissions only to provide its functionality:

  • activeTab – to read subtitles from the currently opened YouTube video;
  • scripting – to inject the translation interface.

No data is transmitted outside the user’s browser.

3. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use analytics, advertising systems, or third-party tracking services.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties, because the Extension does not collect any user information.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will always be available on this page.
